# Tough Decisions Prompts

## Either-Or Decision Template

```
I need to make a difficult decision between two paths.

Option A: [Describe the option]
- Pros: [What's good about it]
- Cons: [What's risky or bad]
- Who supports it: [Stakeholders]

Option B: [Describe the option]
- Pros: [What's good about it]
- Cons: [What's risky or bad]
- Who supports it: [Stakeholders]

Context:
- Why this matters: [Stakes and impact]
- Timeline: [When I need to decide]
- What I can't undo: [Irreversible aspects]

Channel [Persona Name]'s decision-making approach:

1. How would they evaluate these options?
2. What criteria matters most from their perspective?
3. Which would they choose and why?
4. What's a third option I might be missing?
5. How do I communicate this decision once made?
```

## High-Stakes Decision Template

```
I'm facing a decision with significant consequences.

The Decision:
[What I need to decide]

What Makes This Hard:
- Competing values: [What's in tension]
- Different stakeholders want: [Conflicting interests]
- If I'm wrong: [What's at risk]
- Pressure I'm under: [Time, politics, expectations]

What I Know:
- [Fact 1]
- [Fact 2]
- [Fact 3]

What I Don't Know:
- [Uncertainty 1]
- [Uncertainty 2]

Channel [Persona Name] to help me:

1. How do they handle decisions with incomplete information?
2. What principles should guide me when there's no clear right answer?
3. What would they do in my position?
4. How do I stay confident once I decide, even if others disagree?
```

## Unpopular Decision Template

```
I need to make a decision that will be unpopular with [team/leadership/stakeholders].

The Decision:
[What I believe is right]

Why It's Unpopular:
- Who will resist: [People/groups]
- Why they'll resist: [Their reasons]
- What they'll say: [Likely arguments]

Why I Think It's Right:
- [Reason 1]
- [Reason 2]
- [Reason 3]

My Concerns:
- [Fear/doubt 1]
- [Fear/doubt 2]

Channel [Persona Name]'s approach to tough calls:

1. Am I right about this, or am I missing something?
2. How do I stand firm on what matters without being stubborn?
3. How do I communicate this decision to minimize backlash?
4. What support or coalition do I need before announcing this?
5. How do I handle the resistance that will come?
```
