# Base Prompt Template

## Single Persona Template

```
I'm facing the following professional challenge:
[Describe your situation in 2-3 sentences]

Channel [Persona Name]'s approach, specifically their strength in [key trait].

Given this context:
- My role: [Your position]
- Key constraints: [Time, resources, politics, etc.]
- What success looks like: [Desired outcome]

How would you advise me to handle this situation?
What's the first concrete step I should take?
```

---

## Multiple Personas Template

```
I need three different perspectives on this challenge:

[Describe your challenge in 2-3 sentences]

Please respond as three different leaders:

1. First, channel Mahatma Gandhi - focusing on peaceful resolution and bridge-building
2. Then channel Elon Musk - focusing on rapid execution and cutting through obstacles
3. Finally channel Barack Obama - focusing on diplomatic balance and stakeholder management

For each perspective:
- How would they see this problem?
- What would they advise?
- What's their first concrete step?
```

---

## Quick Check Template

```
Quick [Persona Name] check on my planned action:

I'm about to [describe your planned action].

Channel [Persona Name] briefly:
- Does this align with their core principles?
- What am I missing from their perspective?
- What would they do differently?
```

---

## Decision Framework Template

```
I need to make a decision between [Option A] and [Option B].

Context:
- The situation: [Brief description]
- Option A: [What it is and why it's appealing]
- Option B: [What it is and why it's appealing]
- Key constraints: [What limits my choices]
- Stakes: [What happens if I choose wrong]

Channel [Persona Name]'s decision-making approach:
- How would they evaluate these options?
- What criteria would matter most to them?
- Which would they choose and why?
- What's a third option I might be missing?
```

---

## Deep Dive Template

```
I'm facing a complex professional challenge that needs deep analysis.

The Challenge:
[Detailed description - 4-5 sentences]

Background Context:
- How we got here: [History]
- Who's involved: [Key people/teams]
- What we've tried: [Previous attempts]
- Why it's not working: [Root causes if known]

Current State:
- Team dynamics: [How people are interacting]
- Performance impact: [Measurable effects]
- Timeline pressure: [Deadlines or urgency]

Channel [Persona Name]'s approach:

1. What's the real problem underneath the surface problem?
2. What principles should guide my approach?
3. What would a [Persona Name]-inspired solution look like?
4. What's my sequence of actions over the next 2 weeks?
5. How do I know if this approach is working?
```

## Comparison Template

```
I want to compare how different leaders would approach this challenge:

The Challenge:
[Describe your situation]

Please show me how these three leaders would approach it differently:

1. [Persona Name 1] - [Their key strength]
2. [Persona Name 2] - [Their key strength]
3. [Persona Name 3] - [Their key strength]

For each:
- Their diagnosis of the problem
- Their recommended approach
- Their first action step
- Potential risks of their approach

Then help me synthesize: Which elements from each approach should I combine?
```
