# Conflict Resolution Prompts

## Team Conflict Template

```
I'm dealing with a conflict between [Team A] and [Team B].

The Situation:
- What's happening: [Description of the conflict]
- How long it's been going on: [Timeline]
- Impact on work: [Delays, morale, quality issues]
- What triggered it: [The spark that started this]

Channel [Persona Name]'s approach to conflict resolution:

1. What's the real issue underneath the surface conflict?
2. How do I create space for both sides to be heard?
3. What's my strategy to rebuild collaboration?
4. What's the first conversation I need to have, and with whom?
```

---

## Individual Conflict Template

```
I need to mediate a conflict between two team members.

Person A's Perspective:
- Their complaint: [What they're upset about]
- Their behavior: [How they're acting]

Person B's Perspective:
- Their complaint: [What they're upset about]
- Their behavior: [How they're acting]

Context:
- My relationship with each: [Manager, peer, etc.]
- Why this matters: [Impact on team/project]
- Constraints: [Can't fire, can't separate, deadline pressure, etc.]

Channel [Persona Name] to help me:

1. How do I approach each person individually first?
2. What questions do I ask to uncover the real issue?
3. How do I facilitate a productive conversation between them?
4. What ground rules do I set for moving forward?
```

---

## Escalating Conflict Template

```
A conflict in my team is escalating and I need to intervene now.

Current State:
- The conflict: [What started as X, now has become Y]
- Escalation signs: [Describe worsening behaviors]
- Who else is affected: [Team morale, other people pulled in]
- Time pressure: [Why this can't wait]

What I've Tried:
- [Action 1] - [Result]
- [Action 2] - [Result]

Channel [Persona Name]'s crisis management approach:

1. What do I need to do in the next 24 hours to stop the bleeding?
2. How do I address this firmly without making it worse?
3. What boundaries or consequences do I need to set?
4. How do I rebuild from here once the immediate crisis is handled?
```
