Use AI to channel the thinking patterns of different leaders. Each persona brings a unique lens:

| Persona | Key Traits | Best For |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| **Mahatma Gandhi** | Peaceful, Bridge-builder | Conflict resolution, building consensus, healing team rifts |

## ğŸš€ Quick Start

### Step 1: Choose Your Challenge
Pick a specific professional situation you're facing right now.

### Step 2: Select 2-3 Relevant Personas
Based on the table above, choose leaders whose strengths match your challenge type.

### Step 3: Use the Prompt Template
```
I'm facing the following professional challenge:
[Describe your situation in 2-3 sentences]

Channel [Persona Name]'s approach, specifically their strength in [key trait].

Given this context:
- My role: [Your position]
- Key constraints: [Time, resources, politics, etc.]
- What success looks like: [Desired outcome]

How would you advise me to handle this situation?
What's the first concrete step I should take?
```

### Step 4: Synthesize the Perspectives
Don't just take one answer. Compare the different approaches and find your path forward.
